## Critical Gaps

### MH-GAP-002 — 2020 extension GR unavailable in full

- Status: Open; user source requested
- Missing document: Government Resolution dated 25 June 2020 extending the Maharashtra IT/ITeS Policy 2015 until the new policy took effect.
- Authority: Industries Department, Government of Maharashtra.
- Why material: The 2023 policy cites this GR as the bridge between the expired 2015 policy and the 2023 policy. The full text is required for transition, pending applications and grandfathering.
- Search result as of 2026-07-21: The date and effect are confirmed at the beginning of `MH-SRC-2023-001`, but the full GR was not found. The official GR search requires an interactive CAPTCHA.
- Related task: `MH-RQ-002`.

### MH-GAP-003 — No complete 2023 implementation and application pack located

- Status: Open; user source requested
- Missing documents: 2023-policy implementation guidelines, current MAHITI application manual, data-centre registration form, incentive claim forms, FAQs, document checklist and portal workflow.
- Authority: Directorate of Industries; MAITRI; Electronics, Information Technology and Artificial Intelligence Department.
- Why material: Without the operative application pack, the Bible can describe policy eligibility but cannot responsibly state the filing sequence, documentary burden or sanction mechanics.
- Search result as of 2026-07-21: The policy, MAITRI Act, MAITRI Rules, current MAITRI overview and RTS service list were found; a complete data-centre-specific application pack was not.
- Related task: `MH-RQ-003`.

### MH-GAP-004 — 2023 data-centre electricity-duty instrument not located

- Status: Open; user source requested
- Missing documents: Current notification, procedural GR, application form and meter/registration rules implementing permanent electricity-duty exemption for registered data centres under the 2023 policy.
- Authority: Energy Department; Directorate of Industries; Government of Maharashtra eGazette.
- Why material: A policy statement is not sufficient evidence of the operative exemption, the responsible authority or the claim process.
- Search result as of 2026-07-21: The 2017 predecessor procedure (`MH-SRC-2017-002`), 2023 policy text and the current RTS service listing were recovered; no 2023-specific implementing instrument was found.
- Related task: `MH-RQ-004`.

### MH-GAP-005 — 2023 data-centre power-tariff subsidy procedure not located

- Status: Open; user source requested
- Missing documents: Sanction and disbursement guidelines, application form, eligible-consumption rules, budget mechanism, competent-authority order and sample sanction for the ₹1/kWh five-year subsidy outside Zone I.
- Authority: Directorate of Industries; Finance Department; MAITRI.
- Why material: The missing documents determine whether and how the stated benefit can actually be claimed and paid.
- Search result as of 2026-07-21: The 2016 predecessor modalities (`MH-SRC-2016-001`) and a current 15-day RTS service entry (`MH-SRC-2026-001`) were found; no 2023-equivalent procedure was found.
- Related task: `MH-RQ-005`.

### MH-GAP-007 — Green Integrated Data Centre Park implementation pack unavailable

- Status: Open; user source requested
- Missing documents: Official English translation, implementation guidelines, FAQ, application form, selection procedure, empowered-committee or cabinet materials, sanction template and monitoring protocol for the 9 October 2024 amendment.
- Authority: Industries Department; Directorate of Industries; MAITRI; EIT&AI Department.
- Why material: The seven-page GR establishes the policy amendment but does not by itself prove the operational selection, claim and monitoring pathway.
- Search result as of 2026-07-21: The complete official Marathi GR with embedded English provisions was archived as `MH-SRC-2024-001`; no separate implementation pack was found.
- Related task: `MH-RQ-007`.

## Policy and Implementation Gaps

### MH-GAP-001 — Full pre-2015 IT/ITeS policies unavailable

- Status: Open; user source requested
- Missing documents: Complete 1998, 2003 and 2009 Maharashtra IT/ITeS policies and their issuing GRs.
- Authority: Industries Department, Government of Maharashtra.
- Why material: These texts are needed for clause-level historical lineage and to avoid treating later policy summaries as the original law.
- Search result as of 2026-07-21: The 2003-policy extension GR (`MH-SRC-2009-001`) and later official descriptions of the lineage were found; the full policies were not.
- Related task: `MH-RQ-001`.

### MH-GAP-008 — No express reconciliation of the 2025 general policy with the data-centre stack

- Status: Open; user source requested
- Missing document: Official circular, implementation guideline or written clarification stating the hierarchy, concurrency, overlap and customized-package treatment among the 2023 IT/ITeS Policy, the 2024 green-park amendment and the 2025 Industries, Investment and Services Policy.
- Authority: Industries, Investment and Services Department; Directorate of Industries; MAITRI.
- Why material: The three instruments must not be assumed to stack, conflict or supersede one another without an operative savings or implementation rule.
- Search result as of 2026-07-21: All three policy texts were archived, but no express reconciliation instrument was located.
- Related task: `MH-RQ-008`.

### MH-GAP-009 — 21 April 2026 EIT&AI Department notification unavailable

- Status: Open; user source requested
- Missing document: Notification dated 21 April 2026 constituting the Electronics, Information Technology and Artificial Intelligence Department, including transferred functions and delegated powers.
- Authority: General Administration Department and EIT&AI Department, Government of Maharashtra.
- Why material: The notification is required to identify the current policy owner and explain any transfer from the Industries Department or other authorities.
- Search result as of 2026-07-21: The official department introduction (`MH-SRC-2026-006`) cites the notification and states a digital-infrastructure mandate, but does not reproduce the notification number or text.
- Related task: `MH-RQ-009`.

### MH-GAP-010 — Separate continuity and essential-service instruments unavailable

- Status: Open; user source requested
- Missing documents: Operative ESMA or essential-service notification, continuous-power and continuous-water directions, and Dial-before-Dig notification or protocol applicable to data centres.
- Authority: Labour, Energy, Urban Development, Water Supply, Industries and EIT&AI departments, as applicable.
- Why material: These instruments determine whether the 2023 policy's continuity promises have separate legal or administrative effect.
- Search result as of 2026-07-21: The promises are present in `MH-SRC-2023-001`; no separate implementing instruments were found in this pass.
- Related task: `MH-RQ-010`.

## Source-Usability and Conflict Gaps

### MH-GAP-006 — Official stamp-duty Gazette PDF not directly downloadable

- Status: Partially closed; better copy requested
- Missing document: Exact official download of Notification Mudrank-2023/UOR No.20/CR602/M-1 dated 1 February 2024, published in Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV-B, No.54, on 2 February 2024.
- Authority: Revenue and Forest Department; Directorate of Government Printing, Stationery and Publications.
- Why material: The complete locally archived text is from a third-party mirror. The official eGazette catalogue independently authenticates the title, number, part and date, but does not establish byte identity of the mirror.
- Search result as of 2026-07-21: Complete mirror archived as `MH-SRC-2024-002`; official catalogue archived as `MH-SRC-2026-010`; scripted official viewer did not yield a readable full download.
- Related task: `MH-RQ-006`.
