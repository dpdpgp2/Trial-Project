Welcome To Gujarat Fire Safety CoP

Adventure Sport

Welcome to Guj Fire Safety CoP portal. From here, you can access various services like – apply for a new Fire Safety Plan Approval (FSPA), apply for a new Fire Safety Certificate Approval (FSCA), apply for Fire Safety Certificate Renewal (FSCR), building inspection, mock drill and so on simply by logging into this portal. The process is completely paperless and you will receive updates regarding status of your application in real time through email and SMS notifications or simply by logging into your account. The portal also provides for a system of lodging grievances and providing feedback in a transparent manner. Before you start making an application, you should thoroughly read FAQs and Application Guide carefully to know about various aspects like – application forms and checklists, documents required, application fee, role of Fire Safety Officer (FSO) in renewal of Fire Safety Certificate (earlier known as Fire NOC) and so on.
