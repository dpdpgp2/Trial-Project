Electronics Policy (2022-28)

All Electronics units within the Gujarat State are requested to register on the Incentive Management Portal. After registration, you need to login and fill the 'Information Form'. Only those Electronics units who register themselves on the portal and fill the 'Information Form' shall be considered eligible for New/ Expansion/ Diversification during the operative period of the Gujarat Electronics Policy (2022-28).

Biotechnology Policy (2022-27)

All existing Bio Technology units within the Gujarat State are requested to register on the Incentive Management Portal. After registration, you need to login and fill the 'Annexure 1 Form'. Only those Bio Technology units who register themselves on the portal and fill the 'Annexure 1 Form' shall be considered eligible for expansion during the operative period of the Gujarat Bio Technology Policy (2022-27).
