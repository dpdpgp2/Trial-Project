# Gujarat data-centre project-specific source request checklist

Status: 2026-07-19  
Scope: Dholera, Jamnagar, GIFT City, Ahmedabad/Gandhinagar and any other Gujarat data-centre site  
Purpose: distinguish an announcement or general policy entitlement from a legally controlled, permitted, connected, funded and operating project.

## The evidence rule

Request records for the **named project, applicant legal entity, exact plot and specific phase/capacity**. A policy, brochure, master plan, MoU or press release does not prove project execution.

Every supplied record should show, wherever applicable:

- Applicant/SPV's exact legal name and CIN/LLPIN.
- Project name, plot/survey/final-plot number and geospatial coordinates.
- Data-centre IT load and gross connected load, MW/MVA, phase and target COD.
- Issuing authority, file/application number, issue date, effective date and expiry date.
- Conditions precedent, milestones, security/deposit, reporting obligations and default/clawback provisions.
- Amendments, extensions, transfers, cancellations, show-cause notices and current status.
- All annexures, maps, sanctioned drawings, schedules and meeting minutes referenced in the principal record.

If a full document is confidential, request a redacted copy that retains the fields above, the signature page, conditions and annexure index. A screenshot or isolated first page is discovery evidence only.

## Priority-zero request: the minimum bankable project packet

Get these first for each project:

1. Land allotment/lease or conveyance **and** possession/handover record for the exact site.
2. Plot-specific zoning/land-use certificate and sanctioned development-plan extract confirming that a data centre and its power/cooling infrastructure are permissible.
3. Sanctioned grid-load/connection letter, connectivity agreement and phased energisation schedule.
4. Water-allocation/connection letter or bulk-water agreement stating source, daily/peak quantity, quality and availability date.
5. Development permission/building-plan sanction, including sanctioned site plan, FSI computation and all relaxations.
6. Provisional/final fire NOC and Chief Electrical Inspector approval appropriate to the project stage.
7. GPCB Consent to Establish and the environmental/CRZ/groundwater clearance—or a reasoned, project-specific exemption/non-applicability determination.
8. Data-centre-policy application acknowledgement, in-principle approval and final eligibility/sanction order stating approved capacity, eligible fixed-capital investment, benefit caps and conditions.
9. Evidence that each approval remains live: latest extension, compliance filing, construction commencement certificate or commissioning record.
10. For an operating facility: completion/occupancy permission, Consent to Operate, energisation certificate, commercial-operation certificate and incentive-disbursement records.

If even one of items 1–8 is missing, describe the project as **announced/proposed or under diligence**, not fully permitted.

## Project identity and corporate authority

Before requesting sector approvals, lock the identity against which all records must reconcile.

| Record to obtain | Likely source | What it proves / check |

| Certificate of incorporation, CIN and registered office | MCA/company | Exact applicant/SPV identity. |
| Current shareholding and beneficial ownership | MCA/company/investor data room | Sponsor control and group-captive eligibility where relevant. |
| Board resolution/authority approving the project, land and financing | Company | Corporate authority; signatory power. |
| Executed JV, development, lease, operating or service agreement | Project parties | Roles of landowner, developer, operator, tenant and power/water consumer. |

## 1. Land and site-control records

### Records to obtain

- Signed allotment letter, letter of intent, reservation letter and any offer acceptance.
- Registered lease deed, sublease, conveyance deed, licence, development agreement or land-use agreement, with every schedule and site map.
- Possession memo/site-handover certificate and demarcation/survey report.
- Plot number, final-plot number, survey/block numbers, village, taluka, district, coordinates and GIS/KML boundary.
- Current revenue record: 7/12 extract where applicable, Village Form 6 mutation entry, property card/city survey record and record of rights.
- Title-search report and chain of title; encumbrance certificate; registered mortgages, charges, easements and litigation search.
- Land-acquisition/land-pooling/final-plot order and compensation or reconstitution record, where applicable.
- Zoning/land-use certificate, development-plan map extract and applicable town-planning scheme extract.
- Written classification of the data-centre building and associated substation, BESS, DG sets, cooling plant, water plant, tanks and fuel storage as permitted/conditional uses.
- Change-of-use/non-agricultural permission, if required, or reasoned non-applicability record.
- Land premium, lease rent, development charge and infrastructure-charge demand notes and paid receipts.
- Construction-start, minimum-investment, employment, completion and operating covenants in the allotment/lease.
- Performance security/bank guarantee and release/encashment status.
- All extensions, plot substitutions, transfers, subleases, mortgages, notices of breach, cancellation/resumption orders and current no-dues certificate.
- Authority board/committee agenda, minutes and resolution approving the project/allotment.
- Site access, road, utility corridor, fibre/ROW and drainage easements.
- Soil/geotechnical report, topographical survey, flood level and finished-site-level certificate.

### Likely issuers/custodians

- **Dholera:** Dholera Special Investment Regional Development Authority (DSIRDA), Dholera Industrial City Development Ltd (DICDL), applicable revenue/sub-registrar authorities and any state land-owning agency.
- **GIFT City:** GIFT City Company Ltd/GIFT SEZ authorities, landowner/developer, Gandhinagar revenue/sub-registrar authorities.
- **GIDC estate:** Gujarat Industrial Development Corporation and the relevant regional office.
- **Jamnagar/other municipal area:** landowner, district collector/revenue authorities, area development authority/municipal corporation and sub-registrar.

### What the 2012 Dholera GDCR does—and does not prove

The government-hosted Final Development Plan–DSIRDA Report 2 states on its cover that it was sanctioned by GIDB and came into force on 10 September 2012. Section 10.5 establishes a Knowledge and IT Zone and requires an approved Campus Master Plan; Table 10-4 sets plot/FAR/coverage/height controls. It does **not** identify a data centre as a separately defined use, prove a particular plot's zone, or prove that a project obtained development permission. Obtain:

- The gazette/notification or authority resolution implementing the 10 September 2012 sanction.
- Every subsequent GDCR/development-plan amendment and the current consolidated text.
- A certified zoning extract for the exact project plot.
- A project-specific DSIRDA clarification/use-permission expressly covering the data centre and all ancillary plant.
- The approved Campus Master Plan and infrastructure/utilities plan required by section 10.5.2, if the site is in the Knowledge and IT Zone.

## 2. Grid, power and renewable-energy records

### Records to obtain

- Formal load application and acknowledgement showing requested contract demand, connected load, voltage and phase schedule.
- Feasibility/sanctioned-load letter and conditions, including firm/non-firm character.
- Detailed connection estimate, scope split, deposit/security demand and paid receipts.
- Distribution connection agreement and electricity-supply agreement.
- GETCO/STU connectivity approval, bay allocation and transmission-system access approval where EHT/STU intervention is required.
- Load-flow/system-impact study, fault-level study, protection-coordination study and power-quality/harmonics study.
- Approved single-line diagram, metering scheme, relay/protection scheme and earthing design.
- Substation land/site control, route alignment, line/bay work order and utility/EPC construction schedule.
- Primary and backup feed route drawings demonstrating physical diversity; common-mode failure assessment.
- Shutdown/outage coordination letters and target energisation milestones.
- Inspection/test reports, commissioning approval, synchronisation approval and energisation certificate.
- Meter installation/sealing record and first energised bill.
- Tariff category determination, contract-demand billing terms, power-factor/ToD charges and applicable electricity-duty record.
- Open-access application and approval; SLDC registration; wheeling/transmission/banking agreement; scheduling and energy-accounting statements.
- Green Energy Open Access approval and renewable-source identification.
- Captive/group-captive structure: generator shareholding, user equity, voting rights, annual consumption tests and true-up evidence.
- Executed PPA/green-power supply agreement, generator commissioning status, contracted profile and change-in-law/curtailment clauses.
- Renewable-energy certificate treatment and proof supporting any policy green-energy threshold.
- BESS interconnection approval, safety/fire package, capacity and operating rights.
- DG-set approvals, fuel-storage/PESO licence, emissions/noise compliance and runtime limitation.
- Standby supply agreement, restoration priority/SLA and historical feeder/substation interruption data.
- All variations, time extensions, lapsed approvals, cancellation notices and pending network dependencies.

### Likely issuers/custodians

- **Dholera distribution:** Torrent Power Ltd – Distribution (Dholera), plus GETCO/SLDC/GERC where applicable.
- **GIFT City distribution:** GIFT Power Company Ltd, plus GETCO/SLDC/GERC where applicable.
- **Other Gujarat sites:** the relevant DISCOM (UGVCL, MGVCL, DGVCL, PGVCL or Torrent Power), GETCO, Gujarat SLDC and GERC.
- Chief Electrical Inspector/electrical safety authority for installation approvals.

### Data-centre-specific tests

- Reconcile **IT load**, **facility load**, **contract demand** and **transformer/substation MVA**; these are not interchangeable.
- For a 250 MW Dholera claim, obtain the phase-by-phase load ramp, voltage level, dedicated substation/bays, upstream augmentation, responsible payer and ready-for-service dates.
- A tariff order proves available tariff categories, not that a named project has a connection.
- A renewable PPA proves contracted energy, not uninterrupted 24/7 carbon-free supply. Obtain interval-matched supply and curtailment assumptions.

## 3. Water, cooling, drainage and wastewater records

### Records to obtain

- Water-demand application and project water balance for construction and operation.
- Source-allocation/connection letter stating potable, industrial, treated-wastewater or desalinated source.
- Bulk-water supply/utility agreement stating sanctioned average and peak MLD, pressure, quality, delivery point, tariff/escalation, commencement date and curtailment priority.
- Proof that the source and connecting infrastructure are commissioned or funded: intake, treatment/desalination plant, reservoir, pumping station and pipeline status.
- Pipeline route/ROW and connection drawings; deposit and infrastructure-charge receipts.
- Cooling design basis: air/water/adiabatic/liquid cooling, design wet-bulb temperature, PUE/WUE assumptions and phase demand.
- On-site storage volume, redundancy and contingency supply plan.
- Treated-sewage-water/recycled-water supply agreement and quality specification.
- Groundwater abstraction NOC and borewell details, if any; otherwise a written no-groundwater commitment.
- Sewer/wastewater connection permission, accepted quality/quantity and discharge point.
- Cooling-tower blowdown and chemical-treatment plan; effluent treatment/reuse system design.
- DSIRDA grey-water/wastewater permission, discharge-management plan and operational permission where applicable.
- Stormwater-drainage plan, outfall permission, flood-storage calculation and connection approval.
- Commissioning/test certificate for water, sewerage, treatment and reuse systems.
- Water-quality and discharge-test results; inspection notices and non-compliance records.
- Drought/scarcity allocation rules, interruption history and emergency tanker/alternate-source permissions.

### If desalination is claimed

Also obtain:

- Project DPR and ownership/contract structure.
- Source-water intake and brine/outfall design.
- CRZ clearance, environmental clearance/TOR if applicable, marine EIA/model, Gujarat Coastal Zone Management Authority recommendation and GPCB consents.
- Seawater abstraction/intake and outfall permissions, pipeline/ROW/land rights and fisheries/navigation clearances where relevant.
- Plant power demand, tariff/PPA, commissioning schedule, capacity committed to the data centre and take-or-pay terms.
- Brine quality, diffuser performance, monitoring programme and compliance reports.

### Likely issuers/custodians

DSIRDA/DICDL, GIFT City utility entities, GIDC, municipal corporation/local water authority, Gujarat Water Infrastructure Ltd or other named bulk supplier, Gujarat Pollution Control Board, Central Ground Water Authority/state groundwater authority, Gujarat Coastal Zone Management Authority and MoEFCC/PARIVESH as applicable.

## 4. Building, development, fire and operating approvals

### Records to obtain

- Pre-application opinion/use-classification for a data centre and each ancillary facility.
- Development-permission application and final signed development permission.
- Approved Campus Master Plan/subdivision plan where required.
- Sanctioned site plan and all building drawings, with approval stamps and drawing revisions.
- FSI/FAR statement, ground coverage, height, setbacks, parking/loading, access and common-plot calculation.
- Specific relaxation/special-permit orders for FSI, height, parking, DG sets, substations, chillers/cooling towers, tanks, fuel storage, BESS, rooftop plant and telecom towers.
- Commencement certificate and notice of actual commencement.
- Airport Authority of India height NOC, if applicable.
- Structural design basis, geotechnical report, structural-stability certificate and independent peer review.
- Flood-level/site-grading/drainage approval and plinth/finished-floor-level certificate.
- Provisional fire NOC/design approval and final fire NOC, including fire-water storage, pumps, access, compartmentation and emergency power.
- Chief Electrical Inspector approval; transformer, HT/EHT installation and generator approvals.
- PESO licences/approvals for diesel or other regulated storage; lift, pressure-vessel and boiler approvals where applicable.
- Construction-stage inspection reports, deviation notices and regularisation/compounding orders.
- Building completion certificate, occupancy/use permission and phase-wise part-occupancy permission.
- Factory/shops-and-establishments/labour registrations where applicable.
- Data-centre certification reports (e.g., design/constructed/operational standard) only as supplemental evidence; they do not replace statutory permissions.
- Renewals, amendments, revised drawings and current validity confirmation.

### Likely issuers/custodians

DSIRDA for Dholera; GIFT City/GIFT SEZ and relevant authorities for GIFT; GIDC and local area development/municipal authority elsewhere; fire service/State Fire Prevention Services; Chief Electrical Inspector; AAI; PESO and other specialist regulators.

## 5. Environmental, coastal and resilience records

### Core records

- Project-specific environmental applicability memo identifying the applicable EIA Schedule entry, if any, and legal basis for any exemption/non-applicability conclusion.
- PARIVESH Form 1/1A, pre-feasibility report, TOR, EIA/EMP, appraisal minutes and environmental clearance, where applicable.
- GPCB Consent to Establish and Consent to Operate, with application annexures, category/classification and conditions.
- GPCB inspection reports, sampling results, returns, renewals, show-cause/closure directions and prosecution/litigation records.
- CRZ map prepared by an authorised agency, CRZ classification, GCZMA recommendation and MoEFCC/competent-authority clearance for coastal components.
- Groundwater NOC and compliance returns, if groundwater is used.
- Authorisations/registrations for hazardous waste, used oil, e-waste, batteries and other applicable waste streams.
- DG emissions/noise approvals and monitoring; fuel-spill prevention and emergency plan.
- Wastewater/blowdown treatment, reuse and disposal permissions.
- Construction environmental management plan, dust/noise controls and construction-waste authorisation.
- Tree-cutting/transplantation permission and compensatory-plantation record.
- BESS environmental/fire risk analysis and emergency-response plan.
- Site-specific flood, pluvial drainage, storm surge, cyclone, salinity/corrosion, heat and seismic studies using stated return periods.
- Disaster-management/business-continuity plan and emergency coordination record.
- Land-acquisition/social-impact/public-hearing records where applicable.
- Community/fisheries/landowner objections, grievance register, consultation minutes and resolution status.
- Court/NGT/administrative litigation search covering the site, water source, transmission line and coastal works.

### Location-specific emphasis

- **Dholera:** certified site elevation, drainage/outfall capacity, flood-depth modelling, final-plot status, saline/coastal exposure and water-source readiness.
- **Jamnagar:** coastal/CRZ and marine impacts, desalination intake/outfall, industrial-water competition, cyclone/storm-surge exposure and cumulative impacts with the wider complex.
- **GIFT/Ahmedabad:** river/pluvial flood exposure, urban water reliability, heat stress, fire access and utility redundancy.

## 6. Incentive application, sanction and disbursement records

Do not infer an incentive award from policy eligibility or a project announcement.

### Application and approval

- Portal registration and application acknowledgement, including submission timestamp and policy/version selected.
- Complete application and annexure index, including declared project capacity, location, implementation schedule and applicant/operator/developer structure.
- In-principle approval and its deadline/conditions.
- Queries/deficiency notices and applicant responses.
- Site inspection/technical appraisal report.
- High-Powered Committee/State-Level Empowered Committee or other competent-committee agenda, minutes and approval resolution.
- Final eligibility certificate/sanction order identifying:
  - eligible legal entity and project/plot;
  - approved IT load/facility capacity and phase;
  - eligible fixed-capital investment and excluded assets;
  - benefit type, rate, cap, annual cap and claim period;
  - commercial-operation and investment deadlines;
  - employment, green-energy, water/desalination and reporting conditions;
  - anti-duplication/other-scheme treatment;
  - transfer/change-in-control restrictions; and
  - clawback, interest and recovery provisions.
- Customised-package proposal, negotiation record, cabinet/committee approval and signed package agreement where applicable.

### Claim and payment

- Chartered accountant/cost accountant certificates and engineer/valuer asset-verification schedules.
- Invoices, payment evidence and fixed-asset register underlying eligible investment.
- Term-loan sanction and disbursement certificates where interest support depends on debt.
- Commercial-operation/commissioning certificate for each phase.
- Each periodic claim form, scrutiny note, approval order and payment advice/treasury reference.
- Electricity bills and utility credit notes for power-tariff support.
- Electricity-duty assessment, exemption/reimbursement order and payment record.
- SGST assessment/net-tax-paid certificate, reimbursement order and payment record.
- Stamp-duty registration record, exemption certificate/refund order and payment evidence.
- Land, water/desalination, interest, infrastructure or other support sanction and disbursement records.
- Annual green-energy compliance calculation and source evidence, including treatment of open access, captive supply and RECs.
- Annual employment, operation and utilisation compliance reports.
- Audit/inspection reports, short-payment disputes, withheld claims and appeal decisions.
- Extension, variation, phase-transfer or change-in-control approvals.
- Show-cause, suspension, cancellation, clawback/recovery orders and proof of recovery/settlement.

### Likely issuers/custodians

Directorate of ICT and e-Governance/Department of Science and Technology, DST Incentive Management Portal, the competent empowered/high-powered committee, Industries/Finance/Revenue/Energy departments, utility/DISCOM and treasury/tax authorities as specified in the operative policy and implementation guidelines.

## 7. Proof of execution beyond approvals

Obtain independent evidence that the sanctioned project is being built and operated:

- EPC notice to proceed, major works contracts and contractor mobilisation record.
- Lender drawdown and project-account utilisation certificates.
- Dated surveyor/engineer progress certificates and geotagged construction reports.
- Utility works progress: substation/bay/line, water pipeline, sewer and fibre.
- Equipment purchase orders and delivery/installation evidence for transformers, switchgear, generators, UPS, batteries, chillers and servers/GPUs.
- Labour/contractor registrations and site-inspection records.
- Commissioning, integrated-systems testing and reliability test results.
- First utility bills and operating-meter data.
- Customer/lease commitments, rack/IT-load utilisation and carrier interconnection evidence, appropriately redacted.
- Insurance inception and operational-risk survey.
- Phase COD acceptance, occupancy/CTO and incentive COD recognition.

## 9. Where to request the records

Use several routes in parallel:

1. **Company/project data room:** best source for executed agreements, applications, paid receipts, technical studies and confidential approvals.
2. **Issuing authority portal/file inspection:** obtain the signed order and all annexures, not a website summary.
3. **RTI:** request authority-held applications, approvals, minutes, inspection/compliance and disbursement records with a narrow project/date/file scope.
4. **MCA/ROC and stock exchanges:** SPV identity, charges, related-party arrangements and material disclosures.
5. **PARIVESH, GPCB, GCZMA, GERC, GETCO, SLDC and licensee proceedings:** regulatory applications, orders, consent/clearance and system evidence.
6. **Land/revenue/sub-registrar records:** title, registration, mutation and encumbrance.
7. **Authority board minutes, tenders and contract-award portals:** public works and site-infrastructure execution.
8. **Court/NGT databases and assembly/committee records:** disputes, objections, delays and government oversight.

## 10. RTI request template

> Subject: Certified project-specific records for [PROJECT / LEGAL ENTITY / SITE]
>
> Please provide certified electronic copies of all applications, letters, orders, agreements, annexures, maps, minutes, inspection reports, compliance reports, amendments, extensions, notices and payment/disbursement records held by [AUTHORITY] concerning [FULL LEGAL ENTITY, CIN IF KNOWN], the proposed/operating [PROJECT NAME] data centre at [PLOT/SURVEY NUMBER, VILLAGE, DISTRICT/COORDINATES], stated capacity [MW/MVA/IT LOAD], for the period [DATE RANGE]. This includes [LIST THE SPECIFIC RECORD CATEGORIES].
>
> Please provide the file/application number, current status and list of documents on file. If any portion is withheld, cite the specific statutory exemption, provide all reasonably severable non-exempt portions, and identify the appellate authority. Personal identifiers may be redacted; project identity, site, capacity, validity, conditions, official signatories, public expenditure and incentive sanction/disbursement should remain visible where legally disclosable. If the record is held by another public authority, please transfer the request as applicable and inform the applicant.

For large files, ask for inspection and a document index before selecting copies. File separate RTIs by authority so one transfer/rejection does not block the entire packet.

## 12. Evidence-status labels

Use the narrowest defensible status:

- **Announced:** press release, summit statement or media report only.
- **MoU signed:** executed MoU reported or obtained; no presumption of site control or permits.
- **Site controlled:** registered right and possession established.
- **Grid/water reserved:** binding project-specific sanction/allocation exists, but infrastructure may not be ready.
- **Permitted:** material building/fire/environmental approvals are valid for the relevant phase.
- **Incentive approved:** final eligibility/sanction order exists; not the same as payment.
- **Under construction:** independent physical/contract/utility evidence exists.
- **Energised/commissioned:** energisation, completion/occupancy and operating approvals exist.
- **Operational:** customer/IT load and continuing compliance are evidenced.
- **Incentive disbursed:** payment/credit evidence exists.
- **Delayed/lapsed/cancelled:** record the authority document, date and affected phase.

No higher status should be assigned merely because a lower-stage document contains an aspirational target date.
