## Bottom line

The report's central conclusion is substantially sound: the public evidence supports an announced/signed MoU and contemplated feasibility assessment for a proposed L&T Vyoma campus at Dholera, while project-specific land, grid, water, planning, environmental, incentive, financing and construction records have not been publicly verified.

The report is not suitable as a final Bible source by itself. Its `turn...` citation tokens are non-portable, its negative-search conclusions require dated caveats, its incentive discussion omits the dedicated Viksit Gujarat Data Centre Policy launched on 9 July 2026, and one NICDC infrastructure sentence is attributed to Dholera even though it appears in another project's section. The report should be used as a source-discovery map and claim inventory, with the underlying EIDs cited instead.

## Material corrections required before reuse

### 1. Incentive framework

Replace the report's statement that the 2022-27 IT/ITeS policy is the apparent policy home with a layered position:

- the IT/ITeS Policy 2022-27 and amendments are the historical/base framework;
- the Viksit Gujarat Data Centre Policy was officially launched on 09/07/2026;
- `GJ-POL-2026-001` is the official policy booklet, as confirmed by the user; its Vainetra URL is retrieval provenance only;
- the booklet's unresolved `XXXXXX` placeholder and absent notification/GR metadata remain implementation gaps;
- project eligibility, overlap, transition and sanction must be analysed from the official booklet, with the missing timing and claim mechanics caveated until implementation instruments are obtained.

### 2. NICDC DMU sentence

Delete from any future synthesis the proposition that `GJ-PROJ-2026-007` proves road, power and water external connectivity is complete for Dholera. The sentence is located in the Haryana logistics-hub section. For Dholera, use only the page-1 statements about the Activation Area, trunk infrastructure, EC, solar park, expressway, airport and approved rail line.

This correction applies to future synthesis. The supplied report remains preserved unchanged for provenance.

### 3. Portal freshness

Use 07/07/2026 as the Dholera planning-page update date from `GJ-LAND-2026-004`, not 23/01/2026.

### 4. Execution-stage wording

Use “not publicly verified” rather than treating absence from portals as proof that a land/permit/construction milestone has not occurred. The independent feasibility language still justifies the conservative MoU/pre-feasibility classification for public-evidence purposes.
