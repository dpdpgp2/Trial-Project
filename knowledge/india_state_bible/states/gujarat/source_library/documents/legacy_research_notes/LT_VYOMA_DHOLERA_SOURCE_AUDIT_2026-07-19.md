# L&T Vyoma - Dholera source audit

Research cut-off: 2026-07-19  
Case-study status: **MoU / feasibility assessment; no public proof of site control, material approvals, construction or commissioning**

## Executive finding

The defensible current description is:

> On 20 February 2026, Gujarat's Department of Science and Technology and L&T Vyoma signed an MoU concerning a proposed 250 MW green AI-ready data-centre campus at Dholera SIR, associated in government/public reporting with a proposed investment of Rs 25,000 crore and a 2028 operating aspiration. The most detailed contemporaneous report, quoting a Gujarat government statement, says the MoU requires L&T Vyoma to conduct a detailed feasibility assessment covering land suitability, infrastructure readiness, availability zones and sustainability parameters. No project-specific land, grid, water, building, environmental, incentive-sanction, financing, construction or commissioning record was located in the public sources checked through 19 July 2026.

The project must therefore remain classified as **MoU signed / pre-feasibility**, not “being built,” “under construction,” “approved,” “land allotted” or “operational by 2028.”

The public identity search did not locate a separate company/CIN named “L&T Vyoma.” L&T's own rebranding release calls Vyoma its data-centre **business**, formerly L&T-Cloudfiniti. Searches and record requests should therefore cover Larsen & Toubro Limited, the Vyoma/Cloudfiniti brand names and any later-nominated project SPV; the signed MoU must identify the actual contracting entity.

## Why this corrects the public narrative

Several summaries convert the MoU into an unconditional investment or construction commitment. `GJ-PROJ-2026-003` preserves the missing qualification: the quoted government statement describes the next step as a **detailed feasibility assessment**. That language means land suitability, infrastructure readiness, availability-zone design and sustainability were not yet settled by the MoU itself.

The Rs 25,000 crore, 250 MW and 2028 figures should be recorded as **proposed/targeted figures associated with the MoU**, not sanctioned capital expenditure, connected load or committed COD.

## Recommended case-study status line

Use this until stronger evidence is obtained:

**Stage:** MoU signed; detailed feasibility assessment contemplated; land, grid, water, statutory approvals, incentives, financing and construction not publicly verified as of 19 July 2026.

## Upgrade conditions

Move the project to a higher execution stage only when the following minimum evidence appears:

- **Site controlled:** registered land right plus possession.
- **Reserved/approved:** project-specific grid and water sanctions plus material statutory approvals.
- **Under construction:** development permission, commencement record and independent physical/EPC evidence.
- **Commissioned/operational:** energisation, occupancy/CTO, integrated testing and operating/customer evidence.

No public statement, target date or generic Dholera infrastructure claim should substitute for these records.
