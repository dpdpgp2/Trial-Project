Gujarat signs agreement with Larsen & Toubro Vyoma to build AI Data Center in Dholera

The Gujarat Government has signed a landmark agreement with Larsen and oubro Vyoma to build a massive AI Data Center in Dholera Special Investment Region. The agreement was signed today at the India AI Impact Summit 2026 in New Delhi, in the presence of state Science and Technology Minister Arjun Modhwadia.
 
L&T Vyoma will invest 25,000 crore rupees to set up a 250 megawatt Green Data Centre, which is expected to commence operations by 2028. The centre will use clean energy to power high-tech services like cloud computing and advanced data analysis. This project will create numerous new jobs in construction and technology, helping Gujarat become a leader in Artificial Intelligence.
 
To support this goal, the Gujarat Government has already set aside more than 850 crore rupees in this year’s budget for AI and digital services. The new facility in Dholera will attract big international tech companies and help build a modern, digital future for the state.
