An official government news portal reports that Gujarat and L&T Vyoma signed an agreement at the India AI Impact Summit 2026 for a **250 MW green AI-ready data center campus at Dholera SIR**, with **₹25,000 crore** proposed investment, and that **L&T Vyoma would carry out a detailed feasibility assessment** while the state would facilitate infrastructure through relevant departments.
