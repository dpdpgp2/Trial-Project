    L&T Vyoma Signs ₹25,000 Crore MoU For 250 MW Green AI Data Centre in Dholera, Gujarat

    Written by: Team Angel OneUpdated on: 23 Feb 2026, 4:37 pm IST
    L&T Vyoma to develop 250 MW green AI-ready hyperscale data centre in Dholera with ₹25,000 crore investment; unveils Nvidia-backed AI factory plans.
    Share

    Larsen & Toubro has expanded its digital infrastructure ambitions through its data centre arm L&T Vyoma by signing an agreement with the Government of Gujarat to explore a large-scale green hyperscale data centre project at Dholera. The development was announced during the India AI Impact Summit 2026. 

    ₹25,000 Crore Green AI Campus At Dholera 

    The Memorandum of Understanding was signed between L&T Vyoma and Gujarat’s Department of Science & Technology for a proposed 250 MW green AI-ready hyperscale data centre campus at Dholera Special Investment Region. The project carries a proposed investment of ₹25,000 crore. 

    Gujarat Deputy Chief Minister Harsh Sanghavi described the move as a major advancement in AI infrastructure, stating that it strengthens Gujarat’s position as a destination for AI, cloud computing and sustainable digital infrastructure. 

    The facility is designed to support next-generation AI workloads while integrating green energy principles into hyperscale operations. 

    Sovereign Cloud and Nvidia AI Factory Expansion 

    Ahead of the announcement, L&T Vyoma introduced its Sovereign Cloud Platform, positioned as an AI-first cloud ecosystem aimed at enterprises and public sector institutions seeking secure and sovereign infrastructure for AI deployment. 

    In parallel, L&T unveiled a joint venture with Nvidia to develop a sovereign, scalable gigawatt-scale AI factory under the IndiaAI Mission. The plan includes scaling Nvidia GPU clusters to 30 MW capacity at L&T’s Chennai data centre within a 300-acre scalable campus, along with a new 40 MW data centre currently under execution in Mumbai. 

    The AI factory will combine Nvidia’s GPUs, CPUs, networking systems, storage platforms and AI enterprise software stack with L&T’s engineering and infrastructure capabilities. The initiative aims to provide production-grade AI capacity for enterprises, policymakers and global off-takers across sectors such as manufacturing, energy, financial services, healthcare and public services.

    L&T Share Price Performance  

    As of 23 February 2026, at 10:30 AM, Larsen and Toubro Ltd share price is trading at ₹4,400 per share, reflecting a gain of 0.44% from the previous closing price. Over the past month, the stock has gained by 17.35%. 

    Conclusion 

    With a ₹25,000 crore green data centre proposal in Dholera and a gigawatt-scale AI factory strategy backed by Nvidia, L&T is positioning itself at the centre of India’s expanding AI and cloud infrastructure ecosystem, combining sustainability with large-scale computing capacity.
