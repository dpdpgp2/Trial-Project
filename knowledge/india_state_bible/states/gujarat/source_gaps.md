## How to Read This Register

This document records evidence that is missing, incomplete, conflicted, stale or available only in a form that cannot support the desired conclusion. It does not mean the underlying record does not exist. A public-source negative finding is always bounded by the date, repositories and names searched.

Material gaps are also stated in the relevant `state_context.md` section so an LLM cannot answer from an apparently complete narrative while missing a decisive qualification.
